Culture of Afghanistan

Afghans have both common cultural features and those that differ between the regions of Afghanistan, each with distinctive cultures partly as a result of geographic obstacles that divide the country.Every city in Afghanistan has different customs and cultures that give Afghanistan a different look. Therefore, we can compare Afghanistan to a garden that has different flowers.

Afghan culture can be viewed in the following different cities

Kabul

Kabul Architecture

Kabul's various architectural designs reflected the various links it has had with empires and civilisations, particularly being on the ancient trade route connecting India and China with Persia and the West.

The old part of Kabul was filled with bazaars nestled along its narrow, crooked streets, examples being the Mandawi and the Bird Market (Ka Foroshi).

Herat

Herat Architecture

The Arg-e Herat or Citadel of Herat, is a fort located in the center of Herat in Afghanistan. It dates back to 330 BC. Many empires have used the fort as their headquarters over the last 2,000 years, during which it was destroyed and rebuilt many times.
